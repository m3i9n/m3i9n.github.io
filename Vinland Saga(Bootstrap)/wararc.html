War Arc(2005)

Plot

The war arc in Vinland Saga can be rephrased as the introductory section of the series, where the audience is introduced to the main characters and the central themes of the story. This arc primarily focuses on the character of Thorfinn, a young boy who is forced to join a band of Viking mercenaries led by his father, Thors. Through Thorfinn's perspective, the audience is given a glimpse into the harsh realities of Viking life and the violent conflicts that defined this era. The prologue arc also sets the stage for the overarching narrative of the series, which centers around Thorfinn's quest for revenge against his father's killer. Overall, the war arc serves as an effective foundation for the series, establishing the tone, setting, and central conflicts that will drive the story forward.
